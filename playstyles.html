Evolution of NBA Play Styles

By Nikhil Sharma, Chris Robles, and Konner Macias

Problem Description

How has the way basketball is played evolved over time? BSA tackles this question by indicating pivotal moments in NBA history, i.e., Game Changers, which shifted the way basketball is played. To illustrate the styles, we used a clustering based approach to uncover styles of play beyond just their positions (P,SG, etc).
